Sharkie is the friendliest shark ever, but he lives in a hostile environment.

All the other animals he encounters are deathly dangerous for him. Luckily, he found out how to take advantage of the pollution of the sea to defend himself.

Help Sharkie to fight his enemies and survive!

In this level, Sharkie meets even more dangerous animals. Be careful, to stay away from them and fight them from a safe distance!

With the coins collected in the previous level, Sharkie got some cures to improve his health.

How can Sharkie fight his enemies?

Slap pufferfish with Sharkie's fin to get rid of them. Sharkie must be directly in front of or behind the enemey to hit it.

Jellyfish are really dangerous, so keep your distance! The only way to fight them is to trap them in a bubble (no poison needed).

It will take 3 poisoned bubbles to kill the killer whale. Be careful not to waste poison on other enemies or to bubble from far away!
